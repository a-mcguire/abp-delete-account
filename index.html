Livestock PRM App – Account & Data Deletion

This application is provided by ABP Food Group for internal use by authorized employees only.

To request deletion of your PRM account and associated data, please contact the ABP IT Helpdesk at Support.IT@abpireland.com.

When an account is deleted, personal information is removed from the system. Operational records required for business and legal reasons may be retained in accordance with ABP data-retention policies.

For further information, refer to ABP’s internal Privacy Policy or contact ABP IT Support.
